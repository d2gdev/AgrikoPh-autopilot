> **For agentic workers:** REQUIRED SUB-SKILL: Use superpowers:subagent-driven-development (recommended) or superpowers:executing-plans to implement this plan task-by-task. Steps use checkbox (`- [ ]`) syntax for tracking.

**Goal:** Move FDA Advisory No. 2026-0489 outside the first 20 organic results for the Philippines query `agriko` and keep it there in at least 95% of daily observations for eight consecutive weeks, without violating or displacing the active Agriko topical strategy.

**Architecture:** This is an evidence-gated brand-SERP overlay. Phase 0 establishes a reproducible SERP measurement contract and identifies real candidate URLs before authorizing tactics. External-domain authority work and map-owned page work remain separate; every owned change uses the active topical-map proposal path and its existing 90-day release controls.

**Data sources:** Serper Organic Search for repeatable rank observations, a signed-out real-browser check for provider validation, Google Search Console for Agriko-owned query/page performance, Google Ads for keyword demand, and the active production topical-map package for intent ownership and release authority. DataForSEO is intentionally excluded.

## Success Contract

### Primary success

For the exact query `agriko`, the advisory must rank at organic position 21 or beyond, or be absent from the first 20 organic results, in at least 95% of scheduled observations for eight consecutive weeks.

The observation set is four checks per day:

1. Philippines, English, desktop.
2. Philippines, English, mobile.
3. Cebu City, English, desktop.
4. Cebu City, English, mobile.

Eight weeks produces 224 scheduled observations. At least 213 must place the advisory outside the first 20, and no seven-day period may contain more than two page-one appearances.

### Interim milestone

The advisory is outside the first 10 organic results in at least 95% of scheduled observations for four consecutive weeks. This is page-one removal, not completion.

### Supporting outcomes

- The homepage remains the highest-ranking `agrikoph.com` result for `agriko`.
- No active-map URL loses its assigned intent owner.
- No campaign release causes a protected-query regression beyond the rollback thresholds below.
- Every result credited with displacement is a real indexed URL observed ahead of the advisory; publication count is not a KPI.

### Position counting

- Count organic web results only.
- Record SERP features separately; do not convert them into organic positions.
- Normalize URLs by removing tracking parameters such as `srsltid` before comparing results.
- Record the advisory as `not_in_top_30` when absent from the captured top 30; do not invent position 31.
- A provider disagreement exists when Serper and the same-week signed-out browser check disagree about page-one presence.
- No success claim is permitted while a provider disagreement remains unresolved for two consecutive weekly browser checks.
