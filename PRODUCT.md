# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Agriko's internal marketing operator works inside Shopify Admin to review evidence, prioritize work, approve recommendations, and monitor the results of ad, SEO, content, store, and market-intelligence workflows.

## Product Purpose

Agriko Autopilot consolidates current source data and AI-assisted analysis into operator-reviewable work. Success means the UI presents truthful, traceable, actionable states and no external Shopify or Meta change occurs without the required approval and guardrails.

## Brand Personality

Operational, evidence-led, and trustworthy. Copy should be direct and specific enough for an operator to understand the source, status, risk, and next action without inference.

## Anti-references

Avoid decorative SaaS dashboards, ambiguous success states, unactionable queues, stale recommendations presented as current, duplicated work, hidden provenance, and metrics that disagree across surfaces.

## Design Principles

- Live evidence before interpretation.
- One persisted truth represented consistently across every surface.
- Every displayed work item has a clear, safe next action or an explicit non-actionable reason.
- Operator approval boundaries remain visible and fail closed.
- Dense information is acceptable when hierarchy, provenance, and status remain scannable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use Shopify Polaris semantics and target WCAG 2.1 AA. Preserve keyboard operation, visible focus, non-color status cues, descriptive labels, readable contrast, and reduced-motion-safe interactions.
